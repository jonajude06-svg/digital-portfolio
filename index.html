About Me

As the Bachelor of Physiotherapy and Sports and Exercise Science student at Swinburne University, I aim to help professional athletes and sports enthusiasts recover from their injuries as fast as possible in the most comfortable manner. With my background in the pharmaceutical environment and knowledge of health and wellness, I can provide athletes with the physiotherapy they require to recover from their injuries.

A focus on personalised rehabilitation programmes and holistic care for athletes will allow me to enable them to return to their peak performance levels whilst maintaining their health and preventing future injuries.

Target Roles:

  • Sports Physiotherapist
  • Clinical Physiotherapist
  • Rehabilitation Specialist
  • Exercise Physiologist
  • Sports & Exercise Scientist
