Evidence #3: Sports Injury Rehabilitation Case Study - Grade A

Academic Project

Swinburne University - Advanced Clinical Practice

Completed a comprehensive case study on rehabilitation of a professional athlete recovering from anterior cruciate ligament (ACL) reconstruction. The project included:

  • Detailed Assessment: Full musculoskeletal evaluation and functional movement analysis
  • Evidence Review: Current research on ACL rehabilitation and return-to-sport protocols
  • Intervention Plan: Phased, progressive exercise program tailored to athlete goals
  • Outcome Measurement: Pre- and post-treatment assessments using validated outcome measures
  • Reflection: Critical analysis of treatment effectiveness and patient experience

Outcome: The athlete successfully returned to competitive sport within 9 months, demonstrating the effectiveness of evidence-based, patient-centered rehabilitation.
